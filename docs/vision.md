# Jirow Data Insights

## Vision

To help individuals and businesses understand, forecast and optimize their internet consumption through actionable analytics.

## Mission

Provide clear visibility into mobile data and WiFi usage, enabling smarter decisions and reduced connectivity costs.

## Long-Term Goal

Become part of the Jirow Utility Intelligence ecosystem, combining:

- Internet Analytics
- Electricity Analytics
- Solar Analytics
- Fuel Analytics
- Utility Cost Intelligence

## MVP Goal

Validate demand for internet consumption analytics before investing in cloud infrastructure and advanced features.
