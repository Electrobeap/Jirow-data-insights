## Phase 5 - Jirow Utility Intelligence Platform

### Future Modules

- Internet Analytics
- Electricity Analytics
- Solar Analytics
- Fuel Analytics
- Utility Spending Dashboard

### Vision

A single platform helping Africans understand and optimize utility consumption.

## Strategic Positioning

Jirow Data Insights is not intended to be a standalone data tracking application.

It is the first connectivity analytics module within the broader Jirow Utility Intelligence ecosystem.

The long-term objective is to provide consumers and businesses with a unified view of utility consumption across internet, electricity, solar, fuel, and other critical resources.
